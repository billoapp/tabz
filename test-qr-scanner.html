QR Scanner Test Cases

Test these QR codes with the scanner to verify different formats work:

1. Direct Bar Slug

Simple slug format: sunset-lounge

2. Full URL with Bar Parameter

Complete URL: https://app.tabeza.co.ke/start?bar=sunset-lounge

3. Full URL with Slug Parameter

Complete URL: https://app.tabeza.co.ke/start?slug=sunset-lounge

4. Generic Tabeza URL

Should show "Please scan specific bar QR code": https://app.tabeza.co.ke

5. Invalid/Unknown Format

Should show error: https://example.com/unknown

Instructions:

  1. Open the Tabeza app on mobile
  2. Click "Scan QR or Enter Code"
  3. Scan each QR code above
  4. Verify the correct behavior occurs
